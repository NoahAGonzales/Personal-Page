Closet-hangr

Among the many stresses of life, choosing clothes and managing constantly changing fashions should be the least of a person's worries. Seeing such a problem with students on campus of the University of Texas at Dallas, having to manage school, a social life, and work, Closet-hangr was created.

Closet-hangr tracks clothing consistencies and trends among the current users and tailors clothing recommendations based on such, resulting in truly personal recommendations.

Closet-hangr was built as part of a project-based program organized by the University of Texas at Dallas chapter of the Association of Computing Machinery. It placed 2nd when judged among 8 other groups by 5 experienced judges.

Closet-hangr is not currently deployed, due to the costs of upkeep. I apologize for the inconvenience.
